Eye Care Services


Dr. Kenneth Tsim & Associates Eye Care Services Include:


Comprehensive Eye Exams

Our eye examinations are exceptionally thorough and are designed to be a comprehensive assessment of the visual system and overall eye health. In addition to determining whether you need an update in your glasses or contact lens prescription, Dr. Tsim & Dr. Lum will look for symptoms and signs of ocular disease during your examination.

Eye alignment and Eye muscle testing - this will test your visual coordination and eye muscle control, including your ability to change focus.
Tonometry: Measures the fluid pressure in the eye and is an important test in detecting Glaucoma.
Internal & External Eye Health: A complete internal and external examination of your eyes for signs of possible disorders, such as:
    • Cataracts
    • Glaucoma
    • Macular Degeneration
    • Diabetes
    • Vitreous Diseases
    • Retinal Diseases

Children's Eye Exams

Children require special consideration when it comes to eye exams, as regular eye exams can detect health problems and conditions such as a turned or lazy eye in the early stages.

Diabetic Eye Exams

All patients with Diabetes should have an annual dilated eye exam to detect for any early stages of diabetic ocular changes. Earlier detection will allow for timely treatment which can play a significant role in preventing complete vision loss.

Contact Lens Exams and Fitting

Dr. Tsim & Dr. Lum have had extensive training and clinical experience with the fitting of contact lenses. As every patient has different eye sizes and curvatures, a contact lens fitting involves precise measurements to allow for the proper selection of contact lenses with the correct parameters.

Lasik & PRK co-management

After a comprehensive eye examination, your doctor can discuss with you the different options that are best suited for your prescription and lifestyle, as well as their risks and complications. We are qualified to manage our patients in both the preoperative and postoperative care for the laser vision correction procedure. Dr. Tsim & Dr. Lum work closely with various laser vision centers, including TLC, Bochner and Herzig Eye Institute and can provide your post-operative care in the comfort of our clinic.
